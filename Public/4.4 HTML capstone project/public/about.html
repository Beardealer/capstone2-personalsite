My Hobbies

I am an avid musician; I enjoy playing guitar, drums, bass, writing music and performing with friends at pubs. When I was young I performed on drums at the Royal Northern College of Music, Manchester for Chorley Silver Brass Band. I studied Music Technology at college where I learned music theory to a grade 4 level, and also learned many skills for the recording and production of music, which I still use in my spare time for making my own music.

I also enjoy outdoor activities and sports such as mountain-biking, walking, tennis and camping as well as indoor ones such as video games and reading.
